Our Story

Baking Happiness Since 2015

Bake For Me began as a small home bakery in Kampala with a passion for creating beautiful, delicious cakes that bring people together. What started as a humble kitchen operation has grown into one of Uganda's most beloved bakeries.

Our master bakers combine traditional African baking techniques with modern designs to create cakes that are as stunning as they are delicious. We use only the finest Ugandan-sourced ingredients to ensure every bite is a moment of pure joy.

From weddings to birthdays and everything in between, we're honored to be part of your special Ugandan celebrations with cakes that reflect our rich culture and your unique style.
